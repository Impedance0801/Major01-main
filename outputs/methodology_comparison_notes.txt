Scoring scale used in the comparative methodology figures
0 = not explicitly modeled or measured
1 = indirect, qualitative, or weak emphasis
2 = explicit secondary emphasis
3 = core quantitative emphasis or strongest fit

Comparison basis: Scores are author-assigned from the published methodology descriptions, abstracts, and explicitly reported KPI scopes in the cited studies. They are comparative interpretation scores, not normalized raw performance outcomes.

Scope note: Only papers that present a concrete lean-evaluation workflow were scored. Broad reviews and cross-sectional strategic studies informed the narrative but were not plotted as plant-level methodology templates.
